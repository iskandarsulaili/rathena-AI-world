# AI Legends Bloodline Profiles

This document provides information about the bloodlines available in the AI Legends system.

## Dragon Ancestry

**Element:** Fire  
**Affinity:** Dragon  
**Primary Stat:** STR  
**Secondary Stat:** INT  

### Description
The blood of ancient dragons flows through your veins, granting you power over fire and a connection to these mighty creatures.

### Skills
1. **Dragon's Breath** - Channel fire to damage enemies in an area.
2. **Scales of Protection** - Reduce incoming damage.
3. **Draconic Might** - Passive strength enhancement.
4. **Winged Ascent** - Limited flight ability.
5. **Dragon's Roar** - Intimidate enemies, reducing their stats.

### Unlock Requirements
- Character Level: 40
- STR: 50
- Complete Quest: "Legend of the Ancients"

## Celestial Lineage

**Element:** Holy  
**Affinity:** Star  
**Primary Stat:** INT  
**Secondary Stat:** LUK  

### Description
Your ancestors were blessed by the stars, giving you cosmic powers and insight into the mysteries of the universe.

### Skills
1. **Starfall** - Call down stars to damage enemies.
2. **Cosmic Insight** - Increase accuracy and critical rate.
3. **Astral Blessing** - Heal yourself and allies.
4. **Constellation** - Create a damaging field.
5. **Celestial Guidance** - Reveal hidden things and increase drop rates.

### Unlock Requirements
- Character Level: 40
- INT: 50
- Complete Quest: "Legend of the Ancients"

## Shadow Bloodline

**Element:** Shadow  
**Affinity:** Darkness  
**Primary Stat:** AGI  
**Secondary Stat:** DEX  

### Description
Darkness runs in your family, providing stealth and control over shadows and the night.

### Skills
1. **Shadow Step** - Teleport a short distance.
2. **Cloak of Darkness** - Become invisible.
3. **Night Vision** - See in darkness and detect hidden enemies.
4. **Shadow Strike** - Attack with bonus damage from stealth.
5. **Umbral Form** - Transform into shadow, gaining damage reduction and movement speed.

### Unlock Requirements
- Character Level: 40
- AGI: 50
- Complete Quest: "Legend of the Ancients"

## Nature's Chosen

**Element:** Earth  
**Affinity:** Nature  
**Primary Stat:** VIT  
**Secondary Stat:** STR  

### Description
The spirits of nature have selected your lineage to channel their power, giving you control over plants and animals.

### Skills
1. **Nature's Wrath** - Entangle and damage enemies.
2. **Healing Bloom** - Heal yourself and allies.
3. **Beast Speech** - Communicate with animals.
4. **Verdant Growth** - Create barriers and platforms of plants.
5. **Nature's Blessing** - Regenerate HP and SP over time.

### Unlock Requirements
- Character Level: 40
- VIT: 50
- Complete Quest: "Legend of the Ancients"

## Arcane Heritage

**Element:** Neutral  
**Affinity:** Magic  
**Primary Stat:** INT  
**Secondary Stat:** DEX  

### Description
Magic has been in your family for generations, making spellcasting come naturally to you.

### Skills
1. **Arcane Missile** - Fire magic missiles at your target.
2. **Spell Mastery** - Reduce cast time and SP cost.
3. **Mana Shield** - Convert damage to SP loss.
4. **Spell Absorption** - Absorb enemy spells to restore SP.
5. **Arcane Explosion** - Damage all nearby enemies.

### Unlock Requirements
- Character Level: 40
- INT: 50
- Complete Quest: "Legend of the Ancients"
