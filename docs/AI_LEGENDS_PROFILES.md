# AI Legends Bloodline Profiles

This document provides detailed information about the bloodlines and their manifestations in the AI Legends system, using repurposed existing sprites and models.

## Dragon Ancestry

**Element:** Fire  
**Affinity:** Dragon  
**Primary Stat:** STR  
**Secondary Stat:** INT  
**Base Model:** Warlock
**Effects:**
- Dragon wings effect repurposed from Dragon Knight
- Fire particles from Warlock's Dragon Fire skill
- Glowing effects from Warlock's Crimson Rock

### Description
The blood of ancient dragons flows through your veins, granting you power over fire and a connection to these mighty creatures. As a Dragon Ancestry legend, your character manifests draconic features during skill use - glowing red eyes, scale patterns on skin, and flame effects from modified Warlock skill effects.

### Skills
1. **Dragon's Breath** (Modified Crimson Rock)
   - Channel fire to damage enemies in an area
   - Visual: Dragon head appears using Dragon Knight's breath animation
2. **Scales of Protection** (Modified Warlock's Safetywall)
   - Reduce incoming damage
   - Visual: Shimmering dragon scales using Warlock's barrier effect
3. **Draconic Might** (Modified Power Maximize)
   - Passive strength enhancement
   - Visual: Repurposed Warlock's power aura
4. **Winged Ascent** (Dragon Knight wing animation)
   - Limited flight ability
   - Visual: Dragon Knight's wing effect
5. **Dragon's Roar** (Modified Dragon Howl)
   - Intimidate enemies, reducing their stats
   - Visual: Dragon Knight's roar animation

### Personality Traits
- Proud and powerful
- Values strength and honor
- Natural leader
- Quick to anger but loyal to allies

## Technical Integration

All legend characters utilize existing models and effects with custom modifications:

### Base Models
- Warlock for Dragon Ancestry
- High Priest for Celestial Lineage
- Guillotine Cross for Shadow Bloodline
- Ranger for Nature's Chosen
- Sorcerer for Arcane Heritage

### Effect Systems
- Skill effects repurposed from existing animations
- Particle systems from standard effects
- Aura effects from existing buff visuals
- Transform animations from existing skills

### Animation Integration
- Standard character animations as base
- Modified skill casting animations
- Repurposed buff/state effects
- Enhanced existing particle systems

### Performance Optimization
- Reuse of existing particle pools
- Standard LOD system
- Shared texture atlases
- Cached animation states
